What's real here
This is a clickable prototype, not a live bank. The thesis is real and evidenced; the data is staged so you can see the mechanism.
The thesis in one line

One object (the named Payer) · one loop (every realized payment date retrains the model) · one objective (your financial health — we win when you borrow less).

Invoice data is a commodity in Spain (Verifactu/SII) — so the moat is the closed loop + the objective, not "only we see your invoices."

The regulatory split
core

The non-credit core (truths, tax-sweep, sizing, OCR) is autonomous — outside AI Act Annex III.

grant

The credit grant is human-on-the-loop — Annex III §5(b) high-risk + GDPR Art. 22. A person signs every advance; declines carry a human-signed reason.

The cross-customer payer bureau is roadmap-only · unproven [to validate] — not shown here as a current capability.
