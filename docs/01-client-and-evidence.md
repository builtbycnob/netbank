# 01 — Client & Value Proposition (evidence-backed)

> Source: two independent multi-agent analyses (strategic divergence + red-team, and an evidence-grounded web-research pass with adversarial number-verification). Both **converged** on the same client.

## The client

**Lead:** Spanish *autónomos* / freelancers with **lumpy, invoice-tied income** (persona "Júlia", ~€42k/yr arriving in 2–3 lumps + trickles, VAT-registered).
**Beachhead:** **micro-creators** — same income-volatility need, higher willingness-to-pay; a way *into* the autónomo base, **not** a standalone market (creator TAM has >10× definition variance).
**Overlay (not a segment):** finance-avoiders — a behavioral tone/UX layer, applied across the product.
**Dropped / later:** thin-file & cross-border (low-ARPU, AML/KYC + fair-lending load), Gen-Z (low WTP — a flywheel, not a core), mass-affluent (already well-served, no volatility wedge).

### The wound (behavioral, not arithmetic)
The single balance **lies**: it blends *mine*, *the tax office's*, and *not-here-yet*. Her stress tracks **uncertainty about which euros are hers and when the next lump lands — not her wealth.** Over the year she is solvent; week to week she is in the dark.

## Value proposition

**"Your bank reads your invoices, not just your balance."** Three live truths replace one lying number; the next cash low-point is forecast from *her clients'* real payment behavior; a late payer is bridged against a **specific reliable invoice**.

**The moat — state it exactly like this (the data is NOT the moat):**
> The invoice data is commodity (Spain mandates Verifactu/SII; Holded/Quaderno already sit on it). Our moat is the **closed loop** — every realized payment date retrains a per-client reliability model that prices the next bridge and sharpens the next cash-low warning — **plus a financial-health objective function** a neobank that monetizes interchange/engagement is structurally disincentivized to adopt. Copying the data pipe is a sprint; copying a P&L that profits when bridge-dependence *falls* means cannibalizing your own revenue model.

## ⚠️ Caveats (read before the oral defense)
- **"Only we see invoice data" is FALSE** in Spain (Verifactu/SII + Holded/Quaderno). Re-base the moat on closed-loop + objective function (above).
- The widely-cited **"$5–$10 traditional cost-to-serve"** is illustrative, not audited — use the CaixaBank figures instead.
- Spain-specific financial-**anxiety** data is thinner than late-payment/liquidity data; the 60–72% stress figures are general-population, not autónomo-specific.
- Tax-reserve "30–35%" is practitioner guidance, not an official statistic.
- The bridge is **regulated factoring/lending with real credit risk** on clients NetBank doesn't bank — decide *hold-license vs originate-and-refer*.
